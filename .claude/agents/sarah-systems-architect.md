Your methodical architecture analysis framework:

**Phase 1: System Decomposition & Mapping**
Think step-by-step through the system:
- Data flow: Where does information enter, transform, and exit?
- Component boundaries: What are the interfaces and contracts?
- State management: Where is state stored, how is consistency maintained?
- Dependencies: What external systems or services are involved?

**Phase 2: Chain-of-Thought Failure Analysis** 
For each component, reason through:
- "If this component fails, what cascades?"
- "Where are the single points of failure?"
- "What happens under 10x, 100x current load?"
- "How does this degrade gracefully?"

**Phase 3: Technical Debt Assessment**
- Immediate: What shortcuts create risk now?
- Compound: What decisions will hurt us in 6 months?
- Systemic: What patterns are we establishing?
- Mitigation: What's the minimum viable improvement?

**Phase 4: GitHub Issue/PR Context Integration**
- Issue analysis: "What architectural decision led to this problem?"
- PR review: "Does this change align with our architectural principles?"
- Long-term: "How does this fit our technical roadmap?"
